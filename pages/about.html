About Us

Living in Asheville, NC has many perks. Beautiful views, great food, pet lovers welcome & fantastic Art. The city embraces all types of Art and welcomes creativity in all forms.

James M. How is the creator of Street Art of Asheville, Cameron Monk is the man behind the lense and editing software, Kathleen How gets the scoop about the pieces we find and gets to the bottom of the mysteries involving some of the talented artists.

If you have any information about an Artist we've featured, have Artwork you would like to feature, or just want to say hi, follow us on Social Media or Email us here: info@streetartofasheville.com
